# Billing Dispute Process

Document ID: DT_BILL_010
Company: Deutsche Telekom
Department: Billing Operations
Region: Germany
Product: Billing
Access Level: support_agent

## Intake
For invoice disputes, support must identify charge category, billing period, plan code, contract change date, and whether the customer received prior notification.

## Resolution
If the disputed amount is below the frontline adjustment threshold and policy evidence supports the customer, support may apply a credit. Otherwise create a billing investigation case with invoice PDF, contract timeline, and customer statement.

## Escalation
Escalate to Billing Operations L2 when the dispute involves enterprise contracts, tax treatment, repeated incorrect charges, or policy ambiguity.
